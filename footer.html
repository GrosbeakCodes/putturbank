PUTTUR CO-OPERATIVE TOWN BANK

Under the divine blessings of Sri Mahalingeshwara, Puttur Town Bank was established on 28th October 1909 under the inspiration and leadership of Cooperative Pioneer Shri Molahalli Shivaraya, with a share capital of ₹578 and 33 members. Today, the bank proudly celebrates 116 years of continuous service and success,a true symbol of trust, dedication, and cooperative values.
