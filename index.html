President’s Message

Dear Members, Customers, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to extend my heartfelt greetings to all of you. Our beloved Puttur Co-operative Town Bank Ltd., established with the vision of promoting financial inclusion and community development, has been serving its members with dedication, trust, and transparency for several decades.

Over the years, the Bank has been earning profit continuously since its inception and has grown steadily—both in strength and reputation—thanks to the unwavering support of our valued customers, the team of dedicated employees, and the vision and guidance of our Board of Directors and dynamic Chief Executive Officer.

In this era of digital transformation, our Bank is continuously upgrading its technology and services to offer safe, efficient, and customer-friendly banking solutions. We are committed to maintaining financial stability while ensuring social responsibility, integrity, and customer satisfaction at every step.

As we look ahead, we reaffirm our commitment to progress, innovation, and cooperative values. Together, let us continue this journey towards a stronger and more prosperous future for our members and the community.

Thank you for your continued trust and support.

Chief Executive Officer’s Message

Dear Members, Customers, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to present this brief report on the progress and achievements of our beloved Puttur Co-operative Town Bank Ltd.

Our Bank continues to uphold its proud tradition of service excellence, trust, and transparency in all its operations. With the continued support of our members and customers, the Bank has strengthened its position as one of the leading Urban Co-operative Banks in the region.

We have focused on improving customer service, adopting new technologies, and maintaining financial discipline. The Bank has also introduced digital facilities like Mobile Banking, UPI, POS, NEFT, RTGS, and Cheque Truncation System for faster and safer transactions.

On the financial front, the Bank has maintained a healthy growth rate in deposits and advances while keeping NPAs under control. As per RBI guidelines, our Bank is declared as “Financially Sound and Well Managed (FSWM) Bank” since FY 2023–24.

The Bank’s success is the outcome of the collective efforts of our staff, guidance from the Board of Directors, and the unwavering trust of our members and customers. Looking ahead, our goal is to enhance customer experience through innovation and uphold the cooperative spirit that drives our growth.

With warm regards,

B. Shekar Shetty
Chief Executive Officer
