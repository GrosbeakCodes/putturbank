President’s Message

Dear Members, Customers, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to extend my heartfelt greetings to all of you. Our beloved Puttur Co-operative Town Bank Ltd., established with the vision of promoting financial inclusion and community development, has been serving its members with dedication, trust, and transparency for several decades.

Over the years, the Bank has been earning profit continuously since its inception and has grown steadily—both in strength and reputation—thanks to the unwavering support of our valued customers, the team of dedicated employees and the vision and guidance of Board of Directors and dynamic chief executive officer. Our focus has always been on providing personalized banking services, encouraging savings, and supporting local business and entrepreneurship through need-based lending.

In this era of digital transformation, our Bank is continuously upgrading its technology and services to offer safe, efficient, and customer-friendly banking solutions. We are committed to maintaining financial stability while ensuring social responsibility, integrity, and customer satisfaction at every step.

As we look ahead, we reaffirm our commitment to progress, innovation, and cooperative values. Together, let us continue this journey towards a stronger and more prosperous future for our members and the community.

Thank you for your continued trust and support.

Warm regards,
N. Kishor Kolathaya

Chief Executive Officer’s Message

Dear Members, Customers, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to present this brief report on the progress and achievements of our beloved Puttur Co-operative Town Bank Ltd.

Our Bank continues to uphold its proud tradition of service excellence, trust, and transparency in all its operations. During the past years, we have made steady progress in business growth, profitability, and modernization. With the continued support of our members and customers, the Bank has strengthened its position as one of the leading Urban Co-operative Banks in the region.

We have focused on improving customer service, adopting new technologies, and maintaining financial discipline. Efforts have been made to expand our business base through efficient deposit mobilization and credit disbursement to various sectors including Housing, Vehicles, Small Scale Industries, Jewel Loans, trade, and small enterprises. The Bank has also introduced digital banking facilities to make transactions faster, safer, and more convenient for our customers.

On the financial front, the Bank has maintained a healthy growth rate in deposits and advances while keeping NPAs under control. Our emphasis on compliance, transparency, and risk management continues to ensure financial stability and customer confidence. As per RBI guidelines, our Bank is declared as “Financially Sound and Well Managed (FSWM) Bank” since FY 2023-24.

The Bank’s success is the outcome of the collective efforts of our staff, support and guidance from the Board of Directors, and the unwavering trust of our members and customers. I extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who has contributed to our progress.

Looking ahead, our goal is to enhance customer experience through innovation and technology, strengthen our financial base, and uphold the cooperative spirit that has been the foundation of our growth.

In this era of digital transformation, our Bank is continuously upgrading its technology and providing services like CTS (Cheque Truncation System), Continuous Clearing, Direct NEFT and RTGS, ATM Card, Mobile Banking, UPI/IMPS/POS and other online services. Through the Bank’s own IFSC Code No. UTIB000PCTB, customers can directly transfer funds to their accounts with ease.

Together, we shall continue to build a stronger and more customer-centric Bank that serves as a model for the co-operative sector.

With warm regards,

B. Shekar Shetty
